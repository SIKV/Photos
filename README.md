---

## ✨ Overview

**Photos** is a modern Android app that lets you browse, search, download, and share high-quality free photos from:

- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com)
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com)
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com)

Discover stunning images from talented photographers around the world — all in one place.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🔥 Discover new trending photos daily
- 🔎 Search photos by any topic or keyword
- 🎯 Get personalized recommendations
- ❤️ Save photos to your Favorites
- ⬇️ Download high-resolution images
- 📤 Share photos with others
- 🖼 Set any photo as your device wallpaper

---

## 🏗 Tech Stack

- **UI:** [Jetpack Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose), [Navigation Component](https://developer.android.com/guide/navigation)  
- **Architecture:** [Android Architecture Components](https://developer.android.com/topic/libraries/architecture), [Paging 3](https://developer.android.com/topic/libraries/architecture/paging/v3-overview)  
- **Async & Reactive:** [Kotlin Coroutines](https://kotlinlang.org/docs/reference/coroutines-overview.html), [Kotlin Flow](https://developer.android.com/kotlin/flow)  
- **DI:** [Hilt](https://developer.android.com/training/dependency-injection/hilt-android)  
- **Networking:** [Retrofit](https://square.github.io/retrofit)  
- **Storage:** [Room](https://developer.android.com/topic/libraries/architecture/room), [DataStore](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/androidx/releases/datastore)  
- **Firebase:** [Cloud Firestore](https://firebase.google.com/docs/firestore), [Firebase Remote Config](https://firebase.google.com/docs/remote-config)  
- **ML & Media:** [ML Kit Image Labeling](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit), [Glide](https://github.com/bumptech/glide)
